The Caliph Buskers

Caliph Buskers is a musical group with the status of a music group for the visually impaired (OKU). The group was established
in 2014 with a line -up of group members consisting of Zaki Ismail (Bassist), Fendy Adlun (drums), Nirwan Zainalabidin (Guitarist),
Khairul Azmi Saat (Vocalist/Keyboard) and Nadira Rubiana (Shaker). Start by making a name as a street singer.

Caliph Busker Charity Concert is a community charity which provide the residents who need the help especially the very important daily
needs. It is highlighting regional organizations providing on-the-ground relief in the form of food, shelter, healthcare and more.
The concept of this concert is to hold a charity performance to raise funds through ticket sales and use it to help the local community.
